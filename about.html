Bloom Valley Nursery - About Us

Bloom Valley Nursery

Bloom Valley Nursery is as a local, family-owned plant nursery and gardening supply source. We offer a wide variet of plants, trees and gardening supplies - some of which are hand-crafted on site by our talented associates!
We have a passion for horticulture and love to share that passion with our neighbors. From homeowners simply looking to brighten a room, to the serious horticulturist working on prize-winning blooms, we are here for you.
Stop by sometime and talk to Maggie - she can tell you all about upcycling and sustainability.
