Greenhaven's Bounty - Seed & Plant Exchange

Saturday, April 27th
10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Greenhaven Community Garden - 456 Oak Street, Bloom Valley (Hosted by the Bloom Valley Parks & Recreation Department)
Come join the Greenhaven gardening Community for “Greenhaven's Bounty” - a community seed and plant exchange! Bloom Valley Nursery will be on hand offering expert advice and support, while you connect with fellow Greenhaven residents. Trade & Share: Exchange your extra plants and seeds with other Greenhaven members. Expert Tips: Bloom Valley Nursery staff will be available to answer your gardening questions. Community Vibes: Connect with other Greenhaven members and share your passion for growing. Greenhaven's Tip: Consider bringing a few extra seedlings to trade - we're always excited to help our community grow!

Community Garden Design & Build

Friday, May 24th
6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Greenhaven Community Garden - 456 Oak Street, Bloom Valley (Hosted by the Bloom Valley Parks & Recreation Department)
Get your hands dirty and help shape Greenhaven's future green space! Join the Greenhaven Community and Bloom Valley Nursery for a hands-on Community Garden Design & Build event. Bloom Valley Nursery staff will be on hand to provide tools, materials, and support throughout the event. Design Input: Share your ideas and help us plan the layout. Hands-On Building: Assist with building raised garden beds, preparing the soil, and planting starter seedlings. Expert Guidance: Bloom Valley Nursery staff will be providing tools, materials, and support throughout the event.
